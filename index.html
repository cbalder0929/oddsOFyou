You Already Beat
Impossible Odds.

The odds of you being here, right now, are about 1 in 400 trillion. You are the result of a long line of survivors — ancestors who lived through extinction events, ice ages, and plagues.You are a miracle.

Where You Begin
Shapes What's Possible.

0%

It's easy to fall into the trap of complaining about minor inconveniences, but it's important to remember that most of humanity never gets the chance to experience them. Only about 15% of people alive today were born in first world countries.

Make the most of what you have been given, appreciate and try not to complain.

Education Is Earned,
Not Guaranteed.

0%

Thanks to technology 90% of the world has access to priamary education. 70% have access to secondary education. But only about 7% of the world has access to higher education. The diploma is a privilege, not a right. It is a tool.

The odds are in your favor the question is do you have the discipline and the courage to make it HAPPEN?

Survival Belongs
to the Adaptable.

0%

Roughly 27% of college graduates are lucky enough to end up working in a job directly related to their major. That means you are competing with the 3 other people for the same job.

The new question is are you going to make sure you are the best candidate for the job? Are you going to make sure you are the one that stands out? Or are you going to be the one that blends in with the other 3 people?

The shark does not survive because it is angry. It survives because it never stops moving. It reads the current, picks its angle, and commits.
