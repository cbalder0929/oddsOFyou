Where You Begin
Shapes What's Possible.

Being born is the first miracle. Being born somewhere with clean water, a working grid, and a school down the street is the second — and most of humanity never gets it. Only about 15% of people alive today were born in a high-income country.

Where you started is not your fault. What you do with it is.

Survival Belongs

Roughly 27% of college graduates end up working in a job directly related to their major. The other 73% reinvent themselves — pivoting industries, learning new tools, taking the long way around. The diploma opens the first door. Everything past it gets earned in real time.
